This seemed like a straightforward problem at first, but it's not really.
I thought of somehow counting from list 1 then counting from list 2 in between,
but really the best idea is to merge the lists into a single list.
Because both the lists are already sorted, merging them becomes much easier.
Initially, I thought of iterating over one list, and checking at every iteration of the 2nd list fits
between the current element and the next element...but I soon ran into problems

The correct idea is to iterate over both lists together in a while loop maintaining a pointer for each list (2 pointers),
inserting whichever element is smaller and incrementing the pointer of that list

The loop breaks when one list is exhausted
Afterwards, the remaining portion of the non-exhausted list can be pushed without checking
because the remaining elements will always be >= the elements pushed earlier
